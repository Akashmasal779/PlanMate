About PlanMate

Our Vision

PlanMate is your trusted travel companion, designed to make your journey planning easier, faster, and more exciting. From historic forts and beaches to hidden forest parks and spiritual spots—PlanMate helps travelers discover, customize, and book their perfect trip across India.

Whether you're a student, adventurer, couple, or explorer—PlanMate brings the right tools and inspiration to build unforgettable travel memories. Smart filtering, real visuals, and curated options make trip planning fun and simple.
